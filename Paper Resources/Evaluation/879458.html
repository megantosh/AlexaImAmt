Umfragetexte:
Beschreibung:     Welcome to this study on Usability of Voice Assistants. Thank you for your interest and willingness to take this survey. With the purpose of developing an interactive voice service for the local municipality of Berlin, Germany in mind, your input would help making this service more responsive and accurate. I am interested in knowing how you use voice assistants like Siri, Alexa, Cortana and would like to assess individual conversation flow as well as your experience and general appreciation of a voice assistant.   
Willkommenstext: This study is conducted in the Competence Center Information Retrieval and Machine Learning  Distributed Artificial Intelligence Laboratory at the Technical University of Berlin Participation in this study is confidential. Your data will be anonymized and deleted on 9 October 2018 latest. Your decision to participate is volutary. You can stop at any time and ask to have your data removed. In the event of any publication or presentation resulting form the research, no personally identifiable information will be shared If you have any questions or concerns regarding this study, please contact This survey should take less than 7 minutes and is accessible until 9 May 2018. By clicking Next you agree to participate in this study.      
Endnachricht:     Thank you very much for your time!
